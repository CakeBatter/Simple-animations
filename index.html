My thought on Cubisme

The essay 'Du cubisme' written by both Cubism theorists, Albert Gleizes (1881-1953) and Jean Metzinger (1883-1956). The main point of the book was to demonstrate the use of all space to use as your canvas to give dimension to the audience's perspective. The manifesto appeared in time for the Salon exhibition of the Section d'Or in October 1912 and it can be considered the first aesthetic manifesto of Cubism.

The essentially thing to cubisme is the use of space with the classical method of perspective, to include and intergrate the fourth dimension with 3-D space. In the following pages, I demostrate the visual aspect of 3-D space with this short and simple animations.
